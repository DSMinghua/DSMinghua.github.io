Research Interests

My current research interests include machine learning with performance guarantee, online optimization and algorithms, energy systems (e.g., smart power grids and energy-efficient data centers), intelligent transportation systems, distributed optimization, delay-constrained network coding, machine learning in networked and societal systems, and capitalizing the benefit of data-driven prediction in algorithm/system design.
